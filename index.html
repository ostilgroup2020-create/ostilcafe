Our Story

More than a café —
a community

Ostil Café was born from a simple belief: that a great cup of coffee can change the entire mood of your day. Nestled in the heart of Khaireni, Devdaha-6, Rupandehi, we've built a space where quality meets comfort.

From our carefully sourced beans to our expertly crafted dishes, every element at Ostil is designed to delight your senses. We're not just serving food — we're curating moments.

Our Baristas

The art behind
every cup

Our baristas aren't just coffee makers — they're artisans. Each one has undergone rigorous training and continues to perfect their craft daily. We also offer barista courses for coffee enthusiasts who want to learn the trade.

01
Espresso Fundamentals
Master extraction ratios, tamping pressure, and the perfect 25–30 second pull. Foundation of all great espresso-based drinks.
02
Milk Texturing & Latte Art
Learn microfoam creation, steaming temperature, and pour techniques to craft rosettes, tulips and hearts in the cup.
03
Specialty Coffee Mastery
Advanced brewing methods — V60, AeroPress, cold brew. Coffee origin knowledge, sensory tasting and menu development.
